# Going Deeper

*Twelve Tuesdays, walking the diagnostic, therapeutic, and developmental work of IJH Volume 2.*

The second of the three Fellowship of the Heart series. Twelve ninety-minute sessions for the cohort the Spirit has prepared to keep walking after Getting Started. The work shifts from introducing the Four Connects to the deeper labor those Connects make possible — naming the heart's knots, learning to confess and co-process inside a Safe-Present-Clear-Intentional container, hearing God together as a body, and beginning to read the cohort's own shadow and gift.

These files are the **Companion Lesson Plans** — what the Lead Companion and Co-Companion team hold during the session. The participant-facing materials — the Personal Heart Journal, the Rhythm Card, the Family Conversation Cards — live in [`../shared/`](../shared/index.md) and continue from Getting Started.

## The architecture

Going Deeper is built on **Hebrews 12:1–2** as a single sustained architectural anchor across all twelve weeks. The handbook's Appendix A traces the verse-by-verse mapping into each session — the cloud of witnesses, the weight set aside, the sin that clings, the race set before us, the joy set before Him.

The series has three structural sections:

- **Weeks 1–4** — Re-installation and the working container. The cohort returns from the interlude, integrates new continuers, and learns co-processing — the kind of held conversation that prior weeks only sketched.
- **Weeks 5–8** — Confession, hearing God, and the corporate work. PROAPT becomes a sustained practice; the cohort moves from individuals-hearing-God to the cohort-hearing-God together.
- **Weeks 9–12** — The shadow, the calling, and the bridge. The cohort names what it has been avoiding, each member discerns what is being formed in them, the cohort takes its own honest measure, and the series closes with sending into the interlude before Going Out.

## What each lesson plan contains

The format is the same as Getting Started:

- **Quick Reference Card** — print on cardstock; two copies in the room.
- **WATCH FOR** — the specific risks of this session, named so the team can prepare.
- **Session at a Glance** — aim, anchor scripture, Connect focus, mode, center, between-session practice, IJH source.
- **Pre-Work** for the Companion team and, often, for participants.
- **Materials and Setup** — checklist, room arrangement, pre-session preparation timeline.
- **Detailed Run Sheet** — minute-by-minute flow for the session.
- **Block-by-Block Scripts and Notes** — what the Lead Companion may want in front of them.
- **Between-session practice** — the daily and weekly rhythm participants carry.
- **IJH source** — where the material lives in *Intentional Journey of the Heart*.

Going Deeper sessions hold more weight than Getting Started. Read the handbook (especially Sections 3, 5, and 6) before the series begins. Re-read the **WATCH FOR** and **Crisis Contingencies** sections of each week before walking into the room.
