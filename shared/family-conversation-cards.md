*Forty cards for parents, teens, and the family table*

*John G. Tittle • Pilot edition, May 2026*

# How to use this deck

Forty cards. Ten in each of the four Connects — Self, Others, God, Mission. The cards are not assignments and they are not in order. Pull one when you have ten or fifteen minutes — in the car, at dinner, on a walk, before bed.

## Three ways to use the deck

## 1. Pull one card a week

**Once a week, sit with your teen (or your parent). Pick a card. Read the question aloud. Each person answers, in their own time, in their own way. The card itself does the work — you do not need to add coaching or steer the conversation. Pass on a card if it's the wrong night.**

## 2. Use one when something is in the air

**When something has been off in your family — quiet at dinner, tension on the way to school, energy gone flat — pull the deck. Find a card that fits. Some are written specifically for moments when something needs naming (cards 14, 17, 22, 27).**

## 3. Run the four Connects across a season

**Pick four weeks across a quarter. Week one: pull a SELF card. Week two: an OTHERS card. Week three: a GOD card. Week four: a MISSION card. The deck holds the structural sequence; you don't have to.**

## On using cards well

- The point is the conversation, not the answer. If a card produces five minutes of silence and one honest sentence, that is a complete success.
- Receive without fixing. When your teen tells you something hard, the temptation is to coach. Resist. Most cards work best when the parent listens twice as much as they talk.
- Don't push past honest reluctance. “I don't really want to talk about that one tonight” is a complete answer. Try a different card or come back another night.
- It's okay to draw the same card again later. Some questions deepen on the second or third pass.
- Most cards have a younger version (“If younger —”) for ages roughly 12–14. Use the version that fits.

## On the four Connects

The deck is organized around the four-Connect structure of the IJH Getting Started series:

## SELF (Cards 1–10)

**Connecting with yourself. Soil, story, feelings, fears, what you noticed about yourself this week.**

## OTHERS (Cards 11–20)

**Connecting with each other. Friendship, family, the four conditions of a real container, witnessing, blessing.**

## GOD (Cards 21–30)

**Connecting with God. Hearing, prayer, doubt, scripture, the garden, presence.**

## MISSION (Cards 31–40)

**Connecting with mission. Gifts, calling, uphill and downhill, the shadow, what was prepared for you.**

## Printing notes

This deck is printed two cards per page (with light-color borders to delineate cards). For best use, print on cardstock and cut between cards. Each Connect's cards are color-banded by their callout color: blue for Self, green for Others, gold for God, red for Mission. To make the deck physically more durable, you can laminate after cutting.

# The Cards

## SELF — Cards 1–10

Card 1 • **SELF**

**The first thing you noticed**

*What was the first thing your heart noticed today — before your phone, before breakfast, before anyone else? Just one thing.*

**If younger —** What did you wake up thinking about today?

**Anchor —** *Psalm 139:1–2*

**Use it —** First thing in the morning, or in the car on the way to school.

Card 2 • **SELF**

**Soil check**

*Right now, which kind of soil is your heart most like — hard path, rocky, thorny, or good? Why that one today?*

**If younger —** Is your heart hard, rocky, busy, or open today?

**Anchor —** *Mark 4:14–20*

**Use it —** Sunday evening, before the week starts.

Card 3 • **SELF**

**The thing you don't say**

*Tell me something you've been carrying this week that you haven't said out loud.*

**If younger —** What's something you've been thinking about that you haven't told anyone?

**Anchor —** *Psalm 139:23–24*

**Use it —** Slow car rides. Bedtime. When your child goes quiet at dinner.

Card 4 • **SELF**

**Where you came alive this week**

*When this week were you most yourself? What was happening?*

**Anchor —** *John 10:10*

**Use it —** Friday or Saturday, after a full week to remember.

Card 5 • **SELF**

**Where you went small**

*Where this week did you find yourself making yourself smaller — quieter, more careful, more managed than you actually are?*

**If younger —** Was there a time this week you felt like you couldn't really be yourself?

**Anchor —** *Galatians 1:10*

**Use it —** After a hard social event — school, work, family.

Card 6 • **SELF**

**One footprint**

*Looking back at this past month — one place you can see God's hand more clearly now than you did at the time.*

**Anchor —** *Romans 8:28*

**Use it —** End of the month. A walk together.

Card 7 • **SELF**

**What surprised you about yourself**

*What did you do or feel this week that surprised you about yourself — something you didn't expect?*

**Anchor —** *Jeremiah 17:9*

**Use it —** Weekly check-in. Coffee on Saturday.

Card 8 • **SELF**

**The feeling under the feeling**

*Pick a strong reaction you had this week — anger, frustration, sadness, anxiety. What was the feeling underneath that one?*

**If younger —** When you got really mad or sad this week, what was actually going on?

**Anchor —** *Psalm 42:5*

**Use it —** After a hard day. Once the dust has settled.

Card 9 • **SELF**

**Your story — one chapter**

*If your life were a book, what would the chapter you're living right now be titled? Why?*

**Anchor —** *Psalm 139:16*

**Use it —** Long drives. Thoughtful evenings.

Card 10 • **SELF**

**Where you've been coasting**

*Where in your life have you been coasting lately — going through motions without much heart?*

**Anchor —** *Revelation 3:15–16*

**Use it —** End of a season. New year. Birthday.

## OTHERS — Cards 11–20

Card 11 • **OTHERS**

**Who saw you this week**

*Who really saw you this week? Not who said hi — who actually saw you?*

**If younger —** Who really paid attention to you this week?

**Anchor —** *Genesis 16:13*

**Use it —** Sunday night. Reflecting before the week ahead.

Card 12 • **OTHERS**

**The four conditions in our family**

*Of Safe, Present, Clear, Intentional — which one do we do best as a family? Which is hardest for us?*

**Anchor —** *Ecclesiastes 4:9–12*

**Use it —** Family meeting. Once a quarter.

Card 13 • **OTHERS**

**A friendship that's changed**

*Tell me about a friendship that's looked different this year — closer, more distant, complicated. What's behind the change?*

**Anchor —** *Proverbs 17:17*

**Use it —** Walks. Long Saturdays.

Card 14 • **OTHERS**

**Where I've failed you**

*Where this week have I let you down or missed something I should have caught? It's safe to tell me.*

**Anchor —** *James 5:16*

**Use it —** Parent asks teen. Or the reverse. Approach gently.

Card 15 • **OTHERS**

**What you wish I knew**

*What's one thing you wish I understood about you that I'm pretty sure I don't?*

**If younger —** What's something I get wrong about you a lot?

**Anchor —** *Psalm 139:1–6*

**Use it —** Bedtime. Drive home from somewhere quiet.

Card 16 • **OTHERS**

**The blessing I want to speak**

*I want to bless something specific in you tonight. Here's what I see in you. (Speaker: be specific. Three sentences.)*

**Anchor —** *Numbers 6:24–26*

**Use it —** Birthdays. Big transitions. Or just tonight.

Card 17 • **OTHERS**

**An apology owed**

*Is there something you've been carrying about us — something I did or said — that you haven't named? Now is okay.*

**Anchor —** *Matthew 5:23–24*

**Use it —** When something has been off and neither of you has said it.

Card 18 • **OTHERS**

**The friend you're praying for**

*Who in your life right now needs prayer the most? What is it that's heavy on them?*

**Anchor —** *James 5:16*

**Use it —** Praying together at the end of the conversation.

Card 19 • **OTHERS**

**What you need from me right now**

*What do you actually need from me right now — in this season, not in general?*

**If younger —** What would help you most this week?

**Anchor —** *1 Corinthians 12:25–26*

**Use it —** Beginning of a hard week. After a setback.

Card 20 • **OTHERS**

**The ten-year question**

*What's one thing you hope is still true between us in ten years?*

**Anchor —** *Psalm 78:4*

**Use it —** Year-end. Birthday. Long quiet evenings.

## GOD — Cards 21–30

Card 21 • **GOD**

**Where did you see Him today**

*Where did you see God today — even briefly, even in something small?*

**If younger —** Did you notice God anywhere today?

**Anchor —** *Habakkuk 2:1–2*

**Use it —** Bedtime. Daily. The smallest, most repeatable practice.

Card 22 • **GOD**

**What you say but might not trust**

*Pick a scripture you say you believe — ‘God is good,’ ‘He hears prayer,’ ‘All things work for good.’ Which one do you find hardest to actually trust right now?*

**Anchor —** *Mark 9:24*

**Use it —** Private 1:1. Not at the dinner table. Honest air.

Card 23 • **GOD**

**Your garden**

*If your heart is a garden right now, what's growing? What's overgrown? Where would you not want Jesus to walk — and what might He do there if He did?*

**Anchor —** *John 15:1–5*

**Use it —** Quiet evening. Walks. After Wk 7 of the Getting Started series.

Card 24 • **GOD**

**The prayer you keep praying**

*What prayer are you praying right now that hasn't been answered? How long has it been? How are you holding it?*

**Anchor —** *Luke 18:1–8*

**Use it —** Slow Sunday afternoons. After church.

Card 25 • **GOD**

**What scripture has been saying**

*Has any scripture been chasing you lately — a verse or passage that keeps showing up? What might He be saying through it?*

**Anchor —** *Romans 10:17*

**Use it —** Sunday after church. Mid-week reset.

Card 26 • **GOD**

**Where He surprised you**

*When recently has God shown up differently than you expected? What did that change for you?*

**Anchor —** *Isaiah 55:8–9*

**Use it —** Birthdays. End of a season.

Card 27 • **GOD**

**The quiet doubt**

*What's a small honest doubt about God you don't usually say out loud? Not a faith crisis — just a flicker.*

**Anchor —** *Mark 9:24*

**Use it —** Trusted privacy. After Wk 8 of Getting Started.

Card 28 • **GOD**

**The image of God you carry**

*When you picture God, what's the image that shows up first? Is it the same image you'd want, or different?*

**If younger —** What does God's face look like in your imagination?

**Anchor —** *Hebrews 1:3*

**Use it —** Quiet evenings. Once. Don't push past discomfort.

Card 29 • **GOD**

**What He's been clearing**

*Looking back over the past six months — what is God clearing in you that you didn't choose, but you're starting to see why?*

**Anchor —** *John 15:2*

**Use it —** Half-yearly review. Birthdays. New Year.

Card 30 • **GOD**

**The thing you can't see Him in yet**

*Where in your life can you still not see God's hand — even though you want to?*

**Anchor —** *Romans 8:25*

**Use it —** Honest hard moments. Walks together.

## MISSION — Cards 31–40

Card 31 • **MISSION**

**What the room gets**

*When you're at your most yourself — not performing, not managing — what does the room around you get to be?*

**If younger —** When you're really being you, what's it like for your friends and family?

**Anchor —** *Ephesians 2:10*

**Use it —** Half-yearly. Big decisions. After Wk 9 of Getting Started.

Card 32 • **MISSION**

**Where you came alive this season**

*What activity, conversation, or work this season has made you most alive? Why?*

**Anchor —** *1 Peter 4:10*

**Use it —** End of school year. End of summer. End of a project.

Card 33 • **MISSION**

**What I see in you that you might not see**

*Here is a gift I see in you that you might not have language for yet. (Specific. One sentence. Witnessed.)*

**Anchor —** *Jeremiah 1:5*

**Use it —** Birthdays. Difficult seasons. When they've done something hard.

Card 34 • **MISSION**

**The thing you avoid that you might be made for**

*Is there something you've been avoiding because it scares you — but a part of you suspects you might be made for it?*

**Anchor —** *Esther 4:14*

**Use it —** Big decisions. Standing on a threshold.

Card 35 • **MISSION**

**Uphill versus downhill**

*What is something you're climbing toward (uphill mission)? What flows from you without effort (downhill mission)? How are they connected?*

**Anchor —** *Ephesians 2:10*

**Use it —** Year-end. Vocational decisions. After Wk 9.

Card 36 • **MISSION**

**Where you've been going through motions**

*Where have you been performing — doing the thing without your heart in it? What would it take to bring your heart back?*

**Anchor —** *Colossians 3:23*

**Use it —** Mid-semester. Mid-project. When energy has gone flat.

Card 37 • **MISSION**

**The shadow**

*Every gift has a shadow direction. Where do you suspect you've been using a real gift in a way that mostly serves yourself? What would the redirected version look like?*

**Anchor —** *James 3:13–18*

**Use it —** Older teens, parents. Honest air. Not at the dinner table.

Card 38 • **MISSION**

**Who I've been called to bless**

*Who in your life right now has been put in front of you — someone you have specific capacity to bless? What does that bless-able specifically look like?*

**Anchor —** *Galatians 6:10*

**Use it —** Beginning of a season. New school year. New job.

Card 39 • **MISSION**

**The next step**

*What is one specific small next step toward what you sense God is calling you into?*

**Anchor —** *Proverbs 3:5–6*

**Use it —** When you're stuck. Decision points.

Card 40 • **MISSION**

**What ten years from now wants**

*What is one thing the version of you ten years from now wishes you would start, stop, or commit to today?*

**Anchor —** *Philippians 1:6*

**Use it —** New Year. Big birthdays. Significant transitions.
